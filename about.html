About Aditi Construction Company

Building Excellence Since Establishment

Our Story

Aditi Construction Company was established with a vision to deliver high-quality construction services with integrity and excellence. Under the leadership of Er. Kalika Prasad, we have grown to become one of the trusted names in the construction industry in Uttar Pradesh.

With years of experience in civil engineering and construction, we specialize in infrastructure development, commercial buildings, and government projects. Our expertise in handling civil tenders for prestigious institutions like IIT Kanpur and ALIMCO Kanpur has established our reputation for quality and reliability.

We take pride in our team of skilled engineers, environmentalists, technocrats, and contractors who work together to deliver projects that stand the test of time.

Our Mission

To deliver construction projects with the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility while exceeding client expectations through innovative solutions and timely delivery.

We are committed to maintaining the highest level of professionalism, integrity, honesty, and fairness in our relationships with our suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates, and customers.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected construction company known for excellence in quality, reliability, and innovation in the construction industry.

We aim to create infrastructure that contributes positively to society while maintaining environmental sustainability and providing value to our clients.
