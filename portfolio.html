Featured Project: IIT Kanpur Academic Block

A showcase of our expertise in institutional construction

Project Overview

The IIT Kanpur Academic Block project was a significant undertaking that showcased our expertise in institutional construction. This state-of-the-art facility was designed to provide modern educational spaces for one of India's premier technical institutes.

The project involved constructing a multi-story building with classrooms, lecture halls, faculty offices, and specialized laboratories. We implemented sustainable building practices and used high-quality materials to ensure durability and energy efficiency.

Our team worked closely with the institute's administration to ensure minimal disruption to academic activities during construction. The project was completed on schedule and within budget, meeting all quality standards and specifications.

Project Duration: 18 months
Location: IIT Kanpur Campus
Area: 45,000 sq. ft.
Status: Completed
